# Amplify AI Copilot Submission
Author: Karin Tschudin
Date: June 2026

--------------------------------------------------
1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------
This submission demonstrates the application of Microsoft Copilot across multiple business scenarios within a pharmaceutical supply chain context.

The work focuses on identifying, analyzing, and mitigating critical supply chain risks impacting production continuity and FDA/GMP compliance.  
All tasks are based on a consistent case involving three key components:
- BIND567 (quality instability)
- COMP042 (traceability gaps)
- COMP043 (supplier non-compliance)

The deliverables show how Copilot can be used to:
- Transform unstructured information into structured outputs
- Support decision-making with data analysis
- Create professional documentation and communication artifacts

--------------------------------------------------
2. FILE LIST AND PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------

Task 1
Email_Summary.docx  
Purpose: Executive summary of a critical email, highlighting key issues and recommended actions. 

Supply_Chain_Issues_Review.docx
Purpose: Structured document outlining the business problem, key issues, and defined action items. 

Task 2
Meeting_Invite_Screenshot.png 
Purpose: Outlook/Teams meeting invitation with a structured agenda for stakeholder alignment.

Meeting_Summary.docx 
Purpose: meeting summary document that summarizes the provided Teams Transcript: Addressing Critical Supply Chain Issues in the context of the meeting agenda.

Action_Plan.docx
Purpose: Detailed Action Plan with clearly defined responsibilities and timelines based on the Teams Transcript: Addressing Critical Supply Chain Issues document. Includes the required sections: General Strategies, Immediate Actions, Short-Term Actions (1-3 weeks), and Ongoing Actions 

Task 3
Supply_Chain_Report.xlsx 
Purpose: Data analysis file containing supplier performance metrics, delivery timelines, and risk indicators. 

Supply_Chain_Issue_Analysis_Report.docx
Purpose: Risk assessment report translating data insights into business risks and mitigation strategies. 

Task 4
Supply_Chain_Issue_Compliance_Report.pptx 
Purpose: Executive presentation summarizing key findings, data insights, and mitigation strategy. 

Library of Copilot prompts.docx  
Purpose: Documentation of prompts used to generate and refine all deliverables, demonstrating structured Copilot usage. 


--------------------------------------------------
3. TASK OVERVIEW AND DELIVERABLES
--------------------------------------------------

Task 1 – Email Analysis & Issue Structuring  
Objective:
- Analyze critical email communication
- Identify key risks and required actions

Deliverables:
- Email_Summary.docx 
- Supply_Chain_Issues_Review.docx

Outcome:
- Identification of three major issues impacting quality, traceability, and supplier compliance  
- Structured and actionable recommendations derived from unstructured input


--------------------------------------------------

Task 2 – Meeting Preparation & Action Planning  
Objective:
- Translate issues into a structured stakeholder discussion
- Define actions and ownership

Deliverables:
- Meeting_Invite_Screenshot.png 
- Meeting_Summary.docx
- Action_Plan.docx

Outcome:
- Clear cross-functional alignment  
- Defined action plan including immediate, short-term, and ongoing actions

--------------------------------------------------

Task 3 – Data Analysis & Risk Assessment  
Objective:
- Analyze supply chain data to quantify risk
- Develop insights to support decision-making

Deliverables:
- Supply_Chain_Report.xlsx  
- Supply_Chain_Issue_Analysis_Report.docx

Outcome:
- Identification of supplier performance gaps  
- Risk classification of key components  
- Development of mitigation strategies such as alternative sourcing and safety stock adjustments


--------------------------------------------------

Task 4 – Executive Presentation  
Objective:
- Translate analysis into a concise, executive-level communication format

Deliverables:
- Supply_Chain_Issue_Compliance_Report.pptx 


Outcome:
- Clear visualization of risks and root causes  
- Structured mitigation plan with phased implementation approach


--------------------------------------------------

Supporting Documentation – Copilot Prompt Library  
Objective:
- Demonstrate the structured use of Copilot

Deliverables:
- Library of Copilot prompts.docx  

Outcome:
- Full transparency of prompt engineering approach  
- Evidence of iterative refinement for clarity, structure, and professionalism

--------------------------------------------------

Recommended review sequence:
